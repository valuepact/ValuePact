#What is ValuePact?

A NEW FORM OF CURRENCY
ValuePact is a peer-to-peer digital currency with a distributed, decentralized public ledger, which unlike those of traditional banks is viewable and easily audited by anyone.

AN INNOVATIVE NEW WAY TO TRANSFER MONEY
ValuePact's technology allows people to send and receive fast, easy and secure payments with friends, family, and merchants anywhere in the world. ValuePact allows you to manage your finances without the need for a central authority or bank.
